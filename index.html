(2017-present)
"Dream of me! Hello, we are Dreamcatcher!"

an underrated 7-member girl group from Dreamcatcher Company (formerly HappyFace Entertainment) debuted on
Friday the 13th of January 2017. They're known for their unique discography with their signature use of rock elements
that other KPOP groups rarely used for their songs at that time, their unfiltered personalities, amazing vocals and dance
synchronization, and strong bond between members. They're more popular internationally than in South Korea.

(2018-2021)
"Eyes on me! The moment we become one, everyone pays attention! Hello, we are IZ*ONE!"

a 12-member girl group formed in the reality competition show Produce 48 by Mnet with contestants from AKB48 teams
in Japan and Korean labels. Debuted on October 29, 2018, the strong bond and chemistry between members, had
a lot of various content for fans, dance synchronization, and their signature elegant yet powerful discography is
what makes them loved in the KPOP industry. Unfortunately, they've disbanded in their peak success as their
2.5 year contract ended on April 29, 2021. The members got back to their labels to continue their careers.
They're a super popular group with big number of fans from South Korea, Japan, and China.

(2020-present)
"STAYC girls, it's going down!"

a 6-member girl group formed by the producer duo Black Eyed Pilseung-famous from creating popular title tracks for
TWICE and other KPOP artists-with their founded label High Up Entertainment. Debuted on November 12, 2020,
they've became popular and successful with their teen-fresh style and concepts for their albums,
quirky-sounding title tracks, and their amazing vocals and visuals.
